Hey, I'm Eila. Somehow, you've made your way my blog. Thanks for being here!

About me - I got a bachelor's degree in Aerospace engineering in 2017, and since then have been working in Ottawa as an FPGA designer.

I'm addicted to obtaining new skills: I call it going on a learning quest. The start point is becoming obsessed with a "thing", while having no idea how such a thing is made/done. The journey is gathering all the information and materials I can, in order to try to make/learn the thing. And as of the time of writing this, the end is sharing the process on this blog.

A few of my ongoing learning quests are: building a canoe, learning mandarin, and practicing yoga. Some of my past learning quests have been: making my own gear, fixing bikes, and sourdough. As this blog grows and I write more and learn more about web design, I'll be sure to start organizing posts into the appropriate categories. Stay tuned!
