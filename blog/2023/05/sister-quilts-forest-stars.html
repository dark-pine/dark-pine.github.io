This summer, two of my dear childhood friends, a pair of sisters, are tying the knot to their respective partners. We grew up as neighbors and spent many idyllic childhood moments playing together and roaming around the neighborhood. To celebrate this friendship and their new marriages, I've decided that it would be a lovely wedding present to make a pair of "Sister Quilts", one for each sister.

The Quilts

Photos of the quilts.

The two quilts will be identical in piecing, layout, fabric print, and quilting. However, they will have slightly different colour schemes, in order to keep things interesting.
